Job View

Selecting the Job option will display a list of all jobs that are currently setup in the software where you can double click on a job to see a display of it or right click to see additional options.

  • Jobs are displayed in tiles view, where each job is displayed with a few basic properties like Job ID, Job reference, Customer name, Total parts, Due Date, JOB color status bar etc.

  • Job tile also show the part image as stacked card with <PartName : PartQuantity> information.

  • Using Mouse wheel scroll up and down we can see all parts in the JOB.

Job Details

  • Double clicking a tile or pressing Enter key after selecting a tile changes the view into the Detail Mode.

  • The details page displays the parts list along with the nested sheets (layouts), if any.

  • Use Up/Down arrow key to change the job being displayed in the details page.

  • Scrolling the mouse-wheel over the part-stack highlights the part in part list as well as in the layouts.

  • Selecting a layout highlights the layout as well as all parts nested on the layout in the part list. The highlights automatically go off after a minute.

  • Nest summary:

    • Parts shows Total order items and Total Quantity of the JOB.

    • Sheets shows Total number of sheet nested with Total sheet quantity

  • Work Steps:

    • Bending : Machine and its Order quantity to process.

    • Cutting : Machine and its Order quantity to process.

Additional information

You will also note 3 additional icons will appear on the right of the screen which are Simulate, Goto and Back.

  • Simulate will open the selected item for a more detailed view.

  • Goto will switch the focus and display on the part or the nest that is selected.

  • Back will go back one level from where you currently are for example clicking whilst in simulation will take you back to the main job and from a job will take you back to the main jobs display.
