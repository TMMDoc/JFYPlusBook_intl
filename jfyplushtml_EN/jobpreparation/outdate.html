Out-of-Date

Check Off : Auto-refresh Out-of-date layouts

  • factory → Job → Nest settings

    1. Check Off : Auto-refresh Out-of-date layouts

      • Revised part imported into part library results JOB parts updated with revised part & any active nests In progress or Approval State gets highlighted in yellow color.

  • In Job → Nest page, use Filter Out-of-date and select Yes.

  • This list all out-of-date nests which is in progress or approval state.

  • Right-Click those yellow color highlighted Nest and Click "Refresh Out-of-date".

  • Parts in the Nest are updated with revised part.

Check On : Auto-refresh Out-of-date layouts

  • Factory → Job → Nest settings. Check ON : Auto-refresh Out-of-date layouts

    • In progress or Approval state Nests are automatically updated with revised part.
