Auto Plan

Auto Plan

  • Auto Plan steps:

    • First to plan part in bend machines and then cutting machines.

Bend Plan

  • Bend Plan for 5 Parts each having 10 quantity:

    • Click Plan Job…​ ⇒ 2 Parts(20 Quantity) on 5170 6A, 1 Part(10 Quantity) on 5130X 6A B23 & 2 Parts(20 Qunatity) on TBC-5030-V2

    • Job status now is Bend Planned for 50 Quantity.

    • Work Steps are updated with above bend machines and part quantity.

Nest plan - JOB

  • Click Plan Job…​ → Select any machine. Pmonitor engines starts the Nesting Job.

  • Engines splits the Nesting JOB depending upon job priority, due date and material.

  • Using this option, nesting cannot be done against Material on Machine ie., Nesting Material1 on Machine1 and Material2 on Machine2.

    • Now Job Nesting done with 5 Nests on Machine L49-3kw.

    • Job status is 50 : Cutting queue

Nest plan - Order

  • In JOB detail page, Right-click any part and Click "Same Material". From the JOB all parts, that material parts are selected.

  • Plan Part…​ and select any machine for nest.

  • Now Engine takes nesting JOB and sheets are created for Aluminum-30 on L49-3kw.

  • Aluminum-30 nested on L49-3kw. Steel-15 and Steel-10 nested on Mits ML3015.
