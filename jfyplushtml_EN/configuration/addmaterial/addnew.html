Add New

Sheet management can be accessed by clicking on the factory icon and selecting Sheets will give you an overview of the materials that are set up. Clicking on any of the materials mentioned on the left will show you the sheets, thickness, sizes, and quantities that are available.

To add a new material, click Add new → New Material…​ from Sheets page in Factory.

Enter Material, Raw Material, Thickness and Material parameters. Click OK.

View Material

View option in factory → Sheets have following options:

  • Expand All: Expands all material categories.

  • Collapse All: Collapses all material categories.

  • Show All Materials: Lists all raw materials irrespective of sheet inventory.

  • Show Inventory Materials: Lists only raw materials that have sheet inventory.

Raw Material thickness shown in bold represents sheet availability.

Add Sheet

To add a new sheet, click Add new → New Sheet from Sheetspage in factory. Modify Sheet parameters.

Edit or Delete Material/Sheet can be done before a part is assigned to that Material or Layout got nested to that sheet.
Changing Material name is not possible at this point. Hence, need to recreate a new one.
